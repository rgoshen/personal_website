About me

Who's this guy?

I am a software engineer currently attending Springboard. I am currently living in Tucson, AZ and looking for opportunities to use my new and past skills to help a company succeed. I have 20 years of military experience in teamwork and leadership. This experience has taught me to adapt and overcome any problem I face. For the past 5 years as a geologist for the US Forest Service, I have a strong track record of helping mining companies of all sizes accomplish their goals of exploration and extraction of valuable minerals. I am well versed in working with multidisciplinary teams in order to develop clear and concise environmental analysis documents. I am driven by a strong desire and passion to learn new technologies. This helps me with staying abreast of new mining techniques in order to help the small miner achieve their goals.
